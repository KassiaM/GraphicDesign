About Me

Kassia is currently working at Fractured Rogue Studios. She is always on the hunt for a new challenge and to expand her knowledge. She has experience as an artist, writer and managing global teams. Kassia is fueled by her passion for understanding the nuances in her projects and aims to present a product that her client can be proud of. She continually searches for new technologies and studies to expand her knowledge.

Kassia believes that communication in the workplace is key to success. Establishing a trusting, open line of contact allows the best product that all parties can produce. At Fractured Rogue Studios, she created the key brand look and led the team in establishing consistency throughout their multiple media networks. She also led the creation of detailed technical documents that guided the team on a focused vision.

Kassia is on the hunt for a new challenge that can utilize her varied skills as a writer and artist. Reach out at kassiamsher@gmail.com to connect!
